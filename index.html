About Me

I am a Trainee of ADIT NSTI Bengaluru

I am Uvaish Khan, Trainee of Advance Diploma in International Taxation (ADIT) at National Skill Training Institute, Bengaluru. I teach Trade Theory, Trade Practical, Soft Skills for Computer Software Application . I have completed my Bachalor of Technology in Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) from Punjab Technical University (PTU). NSTI is also known as National Skill Training Institute. National Skill Training Institute, Bengaluru is situated on sprawling 44 acres campus, was setup in 1968 by the Government of India, Ministry of Labour & Employment, Directorate General of Employment and Training (DGE&T) as a pioneer institute for upgrading the technical Skills.
